Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CardioPredict AI and cardiovascular health assessment

How accurate is CardioPredict AI?

Our XGBoost model achieves 74% accuracy based on comprehensive evaluation with over 70,000 patient records. While this represents strong performance for medical prediction, accuracy can vary based on individual factors. The system provides probabilistic risk assessment rather than definitive diagnosis.

What machine learning algorithm do you use?

We use XGBoost (Extreme Gradient Boosting), which outperformed other algorithms including Random Forest, SVM, and Logistic Regression. XGBoost excels at handling complex feature interactions and provides excellent regularization to prevent overfitting, making it particularly suitable for medical prediction tasks.

How do I use the risk assessment tool?

Navigate to the "Health Assessment" page and fill out the comprehensive form with your medical information including age, gender, physical measurements, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, glucose levels, and lifestyle factors. The AI will analyze your data and provide a risk assessment within seconds.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need to provide: age, gender, height, weight, systolic and diastolic blood pressure, cholesterol level, glucose level, smoking status, alcohol consumption, and physical activity level. All fields are required for accurate risk assessment.

Is my personal health data safe?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All data processing happens locally on your device or our secure servers. Personal information is not stored permanently - it's used only for the immediate risk calculation and then discarded. We follow HIPAA-compliant practices for health data handling.

Do you share my data with third parties?

No, we do not share, sell, or distribute any personal health information. Your data is processed confidentially and used solely for generating your risk assessment. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security.

Can this replace a doctor's diagnosis?

No, absolutely not. CardioPredict AI is designed for educational and informational purposes only. It cannot replace professional medical diagnosis, treatment, or advice. Always consult qualified healthcare providers for medical decisions, especially if you have symptoms or health concerns.

When should I see a doctor instead of using this tool?

You should seek immediate medical attention if you experience: chest pain, shortness of breath, fainting, severe dizziness, irregular heartbeat, or any other concerning symptoms. This tool is for risk assessment and health awareness, not for emergency situations or medical emergencies.

How can I reduce my cardiovascular risk?

Focus on lifestyle modifications: maintain a healthy diet, exercise regularly, avoid smoking, limit alcohol, manage stress, and get regular health check-ups. Our tool can help you understand your risk factors, but consult healthcare professionals for personalized prevention strategies.
