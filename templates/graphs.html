Model Details

Dataset: Cardiovascular disease prediction dataset with 70,000+ patient records

Features: Age, gender, height, weight, blood pressure (systolic/diastolic), cholesterol, glucose, smoking, alcohol consumption, physical activity

Preprocessing: Feature scaling, BMI calculation, age conversion from days to years
