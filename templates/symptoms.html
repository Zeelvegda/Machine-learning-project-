Heart Disease Symptoms Guide

Learn about common cardiovascular warning signs and when to seek medical attention

Important Medical Notice

This symptoms guide is for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. If you experience any of these symptoms, especially chest pain or shortness of breath, seek immediate medical attention. Early evaluation can be life-saving.

Chest Pain or Discomfort

Pressure, tightness, squeezing, or burning sensation in the chest. May radiate to arms, neck, jaw, or back.

High Priority

Shortness of Breath

Difficulty breathing, especially during physical activity or when lying down. May occur suddenly or gradually.

High Priority

Unusual Fatigue

Extreme tiredness, weakness, or lack of energy that doesn't improve with rest. May be accompanied by dizziness.

Medium Priority

Irregular Heartbeat

Heart palpitations, fluttering, racing, or pounding sensations. May feel like the heart is skipping beats.

Medium Priority

Dizziness or Lightheadedness

Feeling faint, dizzy, or like you're about to pass out. May occur when standing up quickly.

Medium Priority

Swelling in Legs or Ankles

Edema or swelling in the lower extremities, often worse at the end of the day.

Low Priority

Nausea or Indigestion

Unexplained nausea, vomiting, or indigestion that may be mistaken for gastrointestinal issues.

Low Priority

Cold Sweats

Sudden, unexplained sweating, especially when accompanied by other symptoms.

Medium Priority

When to Seek Emergency Care

🚨 Chest pain lasting more than a few minutes
🚨 Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
🚨 Fainting or loss of consciousness
🚨 Severe weakness or numbness
🚨 Sudden confusion or trouble speaking

Call emergency services (911) immediately if you experience any of these symptoms!

Prevention is Key

❤️ Maintain a healthy diet low in saturated fats and cholesterol
🏃‍♂️ Exercise regularly (at least 150 minutes per week)
🚭 Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption
⚖️ Maintain a healthy weight and BMI
🩺 Regular health check-ups and blood pressure monitoring
😴 Get adequate sleep and manage stress
